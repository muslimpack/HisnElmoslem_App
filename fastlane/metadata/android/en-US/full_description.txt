Hisn Elmoslem is a comprehensive, ad-free, and open-source application that provides all the supplications (Azkar) from the famous book "Hisn al-Muslim".

Key Features:
- Ad-free experience with a clean, simple, and intuitive interface.
- Complete "Hisn al-Muslim" book content with search functionality.
- Customization: Multiple color modes (Light, Dark, Black, Yellow) and font adjustments.
- Favorites: Easily add or remove Azkar from your favorites list.
- Reminders: Set alarms for specific Azkar to never miss your daily remembrance.
- Interactive Experience: Sound and vibration feedback on completion.
- Tasbih: Digital counter for various praises.
- Advanced Features: Control via volume buttons, background screen lock, and session restoration.
